Co-founder of Push Pin Studios:

  • Co-founded Push Pin Studios in 1954, a renowned graphic design firm with a distinctive and influential style.

Founding New York Magazine:

  • Co-founded New York Magazine in 1968 with Clay Felker. Glaser served as the president and design director.
